    Quick Start Guide

    1. Import Templates

    Download the templates below to see the exact column headers the application expects. Make sure not to change the structural header names.

    2. Manual Controls

    • Spawn Electives: Drag an elective from the "Available" column into a Period to spawn a class.
    • Assign Campers: Drag campers from the left roster onto an elective card to assign them.
    • Remove Instances: Drag an active elective card back into the "Available" column to delete it and unassign its campers.

    3. Auto-Assign Engine

    Click the Auto-Assign button to instantly generate a schedule and assign campers. The algorithm automatically respects capacities, minimum periods, and camper choices.

    Pro Tip: Any campers you manually assign before clicking Auto-Assign will be locked into place and the algorithm will build around them!
